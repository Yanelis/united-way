Leadership Circle

Individuals who make a United Way gift of $1,000 or more a year are considered
County Leadership Circle members and are invited to special events in recognition of
their dedication to helping others. When you become a member of the County
Leadership Circle, you also become a member of the United Way of
Miami-Dade Leadership & Endowment Circle.

$

If you are 40 years old or younger and give at the Leadership level, you will be considered a Young Leader, a United Way recognition group offering networking, volunteer and leadership development opportunities.

If you are a woman and give at the Leadership level, you will be considered a Women's Leader, a United Way recognition group offering opportunities for networking, intergenerational volunteerism and more.

Family Gift

If your gift above, combined with the gift of your spouse/parther, totals at least $1000 per year, you will be considered a member of the County Leadership Circle.
